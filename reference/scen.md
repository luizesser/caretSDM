# Bioclimatic Variables

A `stars` object with bioclimatic variables (bio1, bio4 and bio12) and
four future scenarios for the Parana state in Brazil. Data from MIROC6
GCM from WorldClim 2.1 at 10 arc-min resolution.

## Usage

``` r
scen
```

## Format

\## \`scen\` A `stars` with 4 attribute and 3 bands:

- ca_ssp245_2090:

  Intermediate scenario for the year 2090 and GCM CanESM5

- ca_ssp585_2090:

  Extreme scenario for the year 2090 and GCM CanESM5

- mi_ssp245_2090:

  Intermediate scenario for the year 2090 and GCM MIROC6

- mi_ssp585_2090:

  Extreme scenario for the year 2090 and GCM MIROC6

- bio1:

  Annual Mean Temperature

- bio4:

  Temperature Seasonality

- bio12:

  Annual Precipitation
